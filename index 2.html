⚠ HOW TO PLAY

☣ OBJECTIVE

Evolve your pathogen to infect and kill all 8 billion humans before a cure is developed. Stay hidden, spread globally, then strike.

⚡ DNA POINTS

Earn DNA as your disease spreads. Spend it in EVOLVE to unlock new traits. Devolve traits for a 50% refund.

🧬 EVOLUTION STRATEGY

1. Start with Transmissions to spread fast.
2. Keep symptoms LOW until fully global.
3. Then pile on lethality symptoms to kill.

🌡 CLIMATE MATTERS

Cold resistance to reach Russia & Canada. Heat resistance for the Middle East & Africa. Without it, spread is severely limited.

🔬 CURE WARNING

High severity triggers cure research. Rich countries fund it faster. Buy Drug Resistance and Genetic Hardening to slow it down.

🗺 MAP

Dark green = healthy • Yellow-orange = infected • Red = heavy • Dark grey = mass death. Tap countries for details.
